Content

Home page

I will need to make productive navigation that will be engaging to the viewer. I will also need to separate each type of music into a different genre. I will also need to hook visitors with a good hero image in the front so that they want to stay on the website for a longer period of time. The interesting design and color and font scheme will be enticing to viewers and will allow them to stay engaged in the website. Overall, the main page will just sell the other pages which contain the main idea of the site.

About Page

People will use this site to explore my music interests. They will be able to see the songs that I recommend and learn why I recommend listening to each song or artist. They will also be able to learn about me and who I am. I will need to describe who I am. I will need to describe the purpose of the site and what it is to the viewers of the site.The question that This site answers is, Who is Kaden Layland?. This site also answers the question: What music should I listen to? Visitors on this site will be able to explore who I am and Why they should care about the content of the website. Visitors will come to my site because they will have a more specific and interesting selection of music. Other song browsers do not describe why songs should be listened to. This selection of music is way more specific and will have a more direct selection of music. These songs will allow a visitor to get started on the music they want to listen to. This site will allow viewers to come back and search for more music when they want to explore different songs. Visitors will also come to this site to learn about me. They will be able to find interest in my musical background and they can compare it to theirs. They will learn about why I enjoy listening to music and what it does for me.

Song Page

The visitors will be able to find songs based on what songs, artists, and genres sound interesting to them. The songs picked will be songs that I enjoy and that Introduce them to a new genre of music. There will be links to spotify and youtube music for each song to appeal to multiple audiences. This page will be a way to learn about music and to support some creators that need more attention. To complete these tasks I will need to create many links to many songs. I will need to describe songs and artists. I will also need to provide pictures where needed. I will need to gather songs That I think other people would be interested in listening to. I will also have to gather artists that I think people will be interested in and want to hear more music from. I might also consider separating different songs onto different tabs or sections so that people can find songs based on a certain genre. I might try to add links in the nav that link to a certain point on the page. I will also want to add a search bar that will allow the viewer to find songs. I will also have a page of all songs so that they can all be in the same place for searching.
