About The Author

I am Kaden Layland. I am the creator of Kamus. I am a college student at BYU-I. I am a computer Science Major. I enjoy spending time hanging out with friends and listening to music. I hope that this site reaches others who have a passion for finding new music.

Why should I listen to these songs? The songs that I chose reflect the given genre well. The songs that I list are a great starting point for finding other great songs. These songs are ones that match my music taste. I hope that others will learn about the type of music which I enjoy listening to. I hope everyone has a good time listening.
